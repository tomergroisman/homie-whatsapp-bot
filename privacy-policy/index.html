Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how this WhatsApp bot ("the Bot") handles information. The Bot is a personal, non-commercial tool used to control a private smart home system via WhatsApp messages.

1. What Data Is Collected

The Bot receives WhatsApp messages sent to it, including the message content and the sender's phone number. This data is used solely to process the request and generate a response.

2. How Data Is Used

Message content is used only to understand and execute smart home commands (e.g., turning lights on/off, checking device status). No data is used for advertising, analytics, or any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage

Messages are not stored in any database or log. They are processed in real time and discarded immediately after a response is sent.

4. Third-Party Services

The Bot uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Google Gemini – natural language understanding to parse commands

Google Gemini receives only the message text (no phone numbers) to determine the user's intent. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for how they handle API data.

5. Data Sharing

No data is sold, rented, or shared with any third party beyond what is described above. Access to the Bot is limited to a small group of family and friends personally invited by the owner.

6. User Rights

If you wish to stop using the Bot or have any questions about your data, please contact the owner directly via WhatsApp or email.
